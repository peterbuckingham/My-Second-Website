    Safely Remove Sun-Spots

    Our Q-Switch ND YAG Laser Machine removes sun spots by delivering short, high-intensity pulses of light that target the excess melanin in the skin. The laser breaks down the pigment into smaller particles, which are then gradually absorbed and removed by the body's natural processes. This technology is precise, ensuring minimal discomfort and preserving the surrounding skin, resulting in a smoother and more even skin tone.
